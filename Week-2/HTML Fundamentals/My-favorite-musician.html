Anirudh Ravichander

Anirudh Ravichander (born 16 October 1990), also credited mononymously as Anirudh, is an Indian music composer, producer and singer-songwriter who works primarily in Tamil cinema. He has also composed music for a few Telugu and Hindi films. He has won two Filmfare Awards South, nine SIIMA Awards, six Edison Awards and five Vijay Awards.
